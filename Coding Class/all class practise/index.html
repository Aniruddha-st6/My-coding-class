A Brief Overview of Daraz

Daraz is the leading online marketplace in South Asia, with a presence in Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal, and Myanmar. It features a wide variety of products, including consumer electronics, fashion, home appliances, and groceries.
