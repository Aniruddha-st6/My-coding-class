Student of Greenherald

The school was established in 1912 by the RNDM Sisters, Christian Missionary and is the first English medium school in Bangladesh.[2][better source needed] Students graduate by sitting for GCE O Levels and A Levels through University of Cambridge International Examinations.[citation needed] The school offers extracurricular activities such as debating, French, science fairs, art competitions, and has athletic teams for basketball, football, and volleyball. It has a debating club run by the students and teachers and a green world earth club. The students participate in the National Debating Championships in the English language. The school has an annual science fair. The school also teaches French from grade 5. Subject choosing is available from 8th grade
